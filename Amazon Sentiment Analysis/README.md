<h2 align="center">Amazon Sentiment Analysis</h2>
<p align="justify">Amazon is the largest online marketplace which sells more than 12 million products and its catalog is constantly growing. Several customers share their opinions and feedback about these products through reviews and star ratings. Data analysis of the reviews can provide insightful customer information that can be used to make products better. Sentiment analysis that uses natural language processing can be used to gather such insights.
</p>
